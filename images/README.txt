CZN CLEANING CO. — IMAGE GUIDE
================================

Drop your photos into this folder using the exact filenames below
and they will appear on the website automatically.

RECOMMENDED: JPG or WebP format. Under 500KB each for fast loading.


HERO BACKGROUND
---------------
File:  hero-bg.jpg
Where: Full-width background behind the headline and quote form
Tips:  A wide shot of a clean commercial space, office, or job in
       progress works great. Dark or moody photos look best because
       a dark overlay sits on top. Min size: 1600×900px.

       To activate it, open styles.css and uncomment the two lines
       under "HERO BACKGROUND IMAGE" (remove the leading spaces and
       the /* */ comment markers).


SERVICE CARD IMAGES
-------------------
Each image appears at the top of its service card.
Recommended size: 800×500px or wider.

  service-post-construction.jpg  →  Post-Construction Cleaning card
  service-strip-wax.jpg          →  Strip & Wax Floor Care card
  service-carpet.jpg             →  Carpet Cleaning card
  service-commercial.jpg         →  Commercial Cleaning card
  service-windows.jpg            →  Window Cleaning card
  service-moveinout.jpg          →  Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning card
  service-janitorial.jpg         →  Office Cleaning & Janitorial card

If a service image is missing, the card just shows the dark
background color — the card still looks fine without it.


ABOUT SECTION IMAGE
-------------------
File:  about.jpg
Where: Left column of the About section
Tips:  A photo of your team, truck, equipment, or a completed job.
       Recommended size: 800×600px. The site crops it to 4:3 ratio.


TIPS
----
- You can rename photos using Windows Explorer or Mac Finder
- Smaller file sizes = faster page loads. Use tinypng.com to
  compress photos before adding them here.
- WebP (.webp) files are smaller than JPG and work in all modern
  browsers — use them if your camera/phone supports it.
